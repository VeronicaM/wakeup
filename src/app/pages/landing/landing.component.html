Wakeup Call App

Enrich your life through the power of questions

Why Wakeup call App?

  • Cultivate a growth mindset. Whenever something impacts us, either through circumstances, thoughts or feelings, we can either make a choice, or react.
    When we make a choice, we ask questions like: "What do I want? What can I learn?", which usually move us forward.
    When we react, we ask questions like: "Whose fault is it? What's wrong with me?", which usually get us stuck.
  • Consciously create the world of your choosing. Using questions as a powerful tool to adjust direction can really impact your life in a positive way.
  • Reflect and find clarity. Take the time to get clear on what matters to you, or just express appreciation for life through writing.
  • Gain a better understanding of yourself.. Through reading your answers, you can observe changes happening over time, get an idea about how you are thinking, and what effect different thoughts and beliefs have on you.

What can you do with Wakeup call app?

  • Group your question sets and quotes into topics, so that you can better focus on certain areas of your life

  • Search through your collection of inspiring quotes
